Yakushima: Japan's Ancient Forest That Few Tourists Find

A UNESCO-listed island covered in cedar trees that are older than many civilizations, Yakushima sits off the southern tip of Kyushu and sees a fraction of the visitors it deserves. The hike through Shiratani Unsuikyo inspired Princess Mononoke — and it still feels that way.

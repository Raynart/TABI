Glossary

Japan travel terms, decoded.

A static reference for recurring words across TABI's food, stay, shopping, and transport guides.

Transport

IC card

A stored-value transit card or mobile wallet setup used for many trains, buses, and small purchases.

Food

Kaiseki

A seasonal multi-course meal often associated with ryokan and refined restaurants.

Food

Konbini

A convenience store, useful for breakfasts, tickets, ATMs, and small travel fixes.

Stay

Onsen

A hot spring bath with etiquette around washing, towels, tattoos, and quiet behavior.

Food

Otoshi

A small starter served at many izakaya as part of a seating charge.

Stay

Ryokan

A Japanese-style inn, usually shaped by tatami rooms, baths, meal timing, and house rules.

City

Shotengai

A local shopping arcade or street, often useful for everyday food and small shops.

Stay

Shukubo

Temple lodging, usually simple and rule-driven, with a focus on quiet and routine.

Logistics

Takkyubin

Luggage delivery service that can make multi-city routes easier.

Shopping

Tax-free

A visitor shopping process that may require sealed bags and passport handling.
